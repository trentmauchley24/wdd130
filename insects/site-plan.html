Content

Home page

This will introduce us to some subclasses of insects that you can learn about. and give some fun facts to start you of. You will also find links to other materials that may be good for others to view.

Images for the Home page

Habitats, Diets, and Defenses

Here you will learn all that there is to know about the habitat and diet for some insects. You will also learn about the defensive nature of some insects like camouflage and armor. This is a good page for pet owners.

Images for the Page 2

Endangered and Invasive Species

There are many insects to be aware about and endangered ones are a good group to go over so that you are not killing the population. You will also be made aware of some invasive species and how to avoid bringing them into certain ecosystems.
