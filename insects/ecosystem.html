Wolf spiders are an all around hunter. This means that its environment that it lives in can be anywhere. They are usually found in grasslands, meadows, and farms. Assassin beetles are found North America. They will hangout around flowers that attract bees, butterflies, and other insects that are looking for food. Ghost mantises are found in Madagascar and on the continent of Africa. They prefer dried shrubs, trees, and bushes. They use the dried terrain for better camouflage.
